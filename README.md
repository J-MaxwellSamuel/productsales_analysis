# 📊 Sales Performance & Revenue Optimization Analysis

## Executive Summary

This project provides a comprehensive analytics solution designed to improve sales performance and support revenue growth for a multi-channel sales organization.

The company sells products through multiple channels — **Online, In-Store, and Phone** — across several geographic regions and relies on a team of sales representatives to drive revenue.

Using **MySQL Workbench for data cleaning and transformation** and **Power BI for visualization and insight generation**, this project analyzes sales performance across regions, products, sales channels, and sales representatives.

The goal is to enable leadership to make **data-driven decisions** that improve profitability, operational efficiency, and sales strategy while targeting a **15% revenue increase next year**.

## Business Problem

The organization aims to improve revenue growth and operational efficiency but currently lacks a unified analytical view of its sales performance.

Key challenges include:

- Inconsistent performance across sales representatives  
- Limited visibility into underperforming regions or products  
- Unclear understanding of how sales channels contribute to overall revenue  
- Difficulty identifying opportunities to increase revenue efficiently
